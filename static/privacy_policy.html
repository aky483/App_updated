At CVCraft, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI-powered resume optimization service.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and login credentials
  • Resume Content: Your uploaded resume files and generated optimized versions
  • Job Description Data: Job descriptions you provide for optimization
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service, including features used and time spent
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Session data and analytics
  • Error logs and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Resume Optimization: Process your resume and job descriptions to provide AI-powered optimization
  • Account Management: Maintain your account, process payments, and provide customer support
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our AI algorithms and user experience
  • Communication: Send important updates about your account and service changes

AI Processing

Your resume content is processed by Google's Gemini AI to provide optimization suggestions. This processing is performed in accordance with Google's privacy policies and our data protection measures.

3. Data Security & Protection

Security Measures:
  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Resume files are processed in secure, isolated environments
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data backups are encrypted and stored securely

Document Handling

  • Temporary Processing: Uploaded resumes are processed temporarily for optimization
  • Secure Storage: Generated CVs are stored securely in your account
  • Data Retention: Files are retained for service functionality but can be deleted upon request
  • No Sharing: Your resume content is never shared with third parties

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Employers or recruiters
  • Marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms

Limited Third-Party Services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For resume optimization processing only
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Database Provider: For secure data storage
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics only

5. Your Rights & Control

You Have the Right To:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion:

  • All resume files and generated content are permanently removed
  • Personal information is deleted from our systems
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement

6. Cookies & Analytics

Essential Cookies

  • Session management and authentication
  • User preferences and settings
  • Security and fraud prevention

Optional Analytics

  • Usage patterns and feature adoption
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking and debugging

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 2 years for quality assurance

8. International Users

CVCraft is hosted in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to the US. We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR for EU users.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users.

11. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users. We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.
