Step 1: Login

Step 2: Upload Your Resume

  1. Go to the "Match Me to Job" tab
  2. Click "Choose your resume file"
  3. Upload your resume in PDF or DOCX format
  4. Wait for the file to be processed

Step 3: Paste Job Description

  1. Copy the complete job description from the job posting
  2. Paste it into the "Job Description" text area
  3. Include requirements, responsibilities, and preferred qualifications
  4. The more detailed, the better the optimization

Step 4: Customize Settings

  1. Set your target ATS match percentage (70-95%)
  2. Choose your preferred template from the sidebar
  3. Select which sections to include/exclude
  4. Adjust advanced options if needed

Step 5: Generate & Download

  1. Click "Generate Optimized CV" button
  2. Wait 15-30 seconds for processing
  3. Review your CV in the "CV Preview" tab
  4. Download in PDF or Word format

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does CV optimization take?

Our AI typically processes your resume in 15-30 seconds. The exact time depends on the length of your resume and job description.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF and DOCX (Word) formats for upload. Generated CVs can be downloaded in both PDF and Word formats.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your resume data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I edit the generated CV?

The generated CV is optimized for ATS systems. You can download it in Word format to make manual edits if needed.

What's the 2-page limit?

All generated CVs are automatically optimized to fit exactly 2 pages - the preferred length for most employers and ATS systems.

Do I need different CVs for different jobs?

Yes! Each job posting has unique requirements. We recommend creating a customized CV for each application for best results.
